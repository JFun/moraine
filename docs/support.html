Frequently asked questions

How do I play?
Swipe up, down, left, or right to set the direction gravity pulls. Every loose block slides that way and stacks, and when a row or column fills completely it clears. Empty the board — or clear every glowing block — in as few swipes as you can, across all 30 hand-designed levels. No accounts, no sign-in — just swipe and play.
Does it work offline?
Yes. Moraine is fully playable without an internet connection. Your progress is saved on your device.
How do I reset my progress?
Delete and reinstall the app to start over. All progress is stored locally on your device, so a fresh install gives you a clean slate.
Are there ads or in-app purchases?
No. Moraine has no ads, no in-app purchases, and no subscriptions.
Is the game suitable for kids?
Yes — it's a calm, non-violent puzzle with no chat, no social features, and no purchases.
